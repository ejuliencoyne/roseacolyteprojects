PROJECTS

A few of the things I am working on now and that I have done over the years.


Pottery (2019 – now)

The ceramics I make are mostly functional, handbuilt, illustrated, and fired in reduction. (There are a few departures from this in my earlier work, though.)

Needlepoint (2023 – now)

Whimsical illustrative needlepoint designs, and a little bit about my design and stitching process.

Quilts (2024 – now) (Page coming soon!)

Mostly improvisational and illustrative quilts with recycled fabrics and hand quilting, as well as quilted tote bags.

Rugs and Punchneedle Illustration (2019 – 2021) (Page coming soon!)

Punchneedle hoops and hand-tufted small rugs. Although I don't do much of this anymore, it was an aesthetic precursor to my needlepoint work.
